Facilities

school library

was built in 2011

The School Library is a welcoming place with a wide range of books suitable for the whole school. From picture books to young fiction and fact books to poetry the Library is a place to read, browse, relax and learn. Supported by our full time Librarian, pupils can use the Library at lunchtimes, during Library lessons and for one-to-one reading throughout the day. Alongside books, the Library also has the weekly newspaper, First News, and the magazines Aquila and Whizz, Pop, Bang and all our resources are aimed at supporting learning and promoting reading for pleasure.



music school

was built in 2011

At St Cedd's School we offer pupils the opportunity to be involved in an impressive array of music-making through our extensive music curriculum and extra-curricular clubs, individual instrumental tuition, membership of an auditioned choir and participation in one or more of our many ensembles. The School is a member of the Choir Schools' Association and we have very close links with Chelmsford Cathedral. Our choristers are members of the Cathedral Choir, and our Junior and Senior Chamber Choirs sing at Evensong. Music is a significant aspect of our learning experience at St Cedd's School and we provide every pupil with the support they need to maximise their potential in this arena.



dance and drama studio

was built in 2015

The Dance and Drama Studio at St Cedd's School provides an extra space to take part in activities and competitions.
